About

Whenever I wanted to share something via a QR-Code, I got pretty annoyed:

Either you need to install something or you use an online tool.

And most of those tools in the top search results are full of ads, collect cookies or even send your inputs to a server. Which you probably do not want, especially when you are creating a QR-Code for an email address or Guest-Wifi-Login.

So I implemented a QR-Code encoder from scratch (for the fun of it):
  • Everything runs in your browser, your data does not leave your device
  • Open Source - Check out the GitHub link
  • No ads, cookies or other annoyances
  • Simple and tiny, loads fast
